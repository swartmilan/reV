reV.offshore.offshore module¶

reV offshore wind farm aggregation module. This module aggregates offshore generation data from high res wind resource data to coarse wind farm sites and then calculates the ORCA econ data.

Offshore resource / generation data refers to WTK 2km (fine resolution) Offshore farms refer to ORCA data on 600MW wind farms (coarse resolution)
